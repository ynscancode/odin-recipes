Chicken Rice Recipe

Home

Ingredients

  • A whole chicken
  • Salt
  • Scallions, trimmed and cut into quarters
  • Unpeeled ginger, sliced
  • Garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Rice

Steps:

  1. Prep the chicken.
  2. Boil the chicken with ginger, garlic, sesame oil and soy sauce
  3. Cook rice in rice cooker.
  4. Ice the chicken.
  5. Put chicken on top of rice and continue cooking rice.
  6. Eat!
