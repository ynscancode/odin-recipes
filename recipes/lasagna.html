Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • Onion, garlic, carrot and celery
  • Beef
  • Canned tomato and tomato paste
  • Red wine - for extra flavour!
  • Seasonings
  • Butter, flour, milk, cheese
  • Lasagna sheets

Steps:

  1. Layer up beef, lasagna sheets and sauce.
  2. Repeat step 1.
  3. Put cheese on top.
  4. Bake!
